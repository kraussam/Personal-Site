My Reads

Economics in One Lesson. Henry Hazlitt 2016
A concise and insightful guide to free-market economics, this book debunks the broken window fallacy, which is the idea that destruction can be good for the economy, and provides a clear introduction to economic principles that anyone can understand. A must-read for those interested in gaining a better understanding of economics and the power of free markets.
Intelligent Investor. Benjamin Graham 2020
A classic guide to value investing, that emphasizes the importance of long-term strategies and analysis of individual stocks and bonds. Graham's approach to investing has made his book a must-read for both beginner and experienced investors, providing valuable insights and practical advice on how to navigate the complex world of investing.
HTML, CSS, and Javascript for Web Developers Johns Hopkins University 2023
"HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for Web Developers" by Johns Hopkins University provided the essential foundation for my website development. The course emphasizes practical examples and hands-on projects for responsive and dynamic web applications, making it an essential resource for developers.
